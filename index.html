Monochrome Natural Earth Globe

This demo renders a minimalist Earth using Natural Earth 1:50m land polygons projected onto a sphere with Three.js. Landmasses are built from GeoJSON data rather than texture maps, resulting in crisp, stylized silhouettes.

  • Natural Earth GeoJSON projected onto a 3D sphere
  • Ambient and directional lighting for soft shading
  • Interactive OrbitControls with gentle auto-rotation
  • Lightweight, texture-free rendering

View the code in main.js to see how the GeoJSON polygons are triangulated and mapped into spherical meshes.
